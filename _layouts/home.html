A tiny design studio in New York City

Cottleston Pie works with organizations that make a positive impact in the world.

We work with organizations to design and build things used on screens. From conception to delivery (and every step in between), Cottleston Pie takes a wholistic approach to digital design.

Specializing in UX & UI Design, responsive web design and development. Available for full projects or as an individual consultant.
